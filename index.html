    Frequently Asked Questions

    Is my issue/project too simple for your team?

    No. We have many clients with simple issues such as contact card scanning or printer setups. We dont mind. We love tech.

    Is my issue/project to complex for your team?

    Unless you're an enterprise-scale company, we can probably help with your issue/project. We are a dynamic team that works on varied projects with different sizes and durations.

    How quickly can you assist me with my issue/project?

    While we book up quickly, we usually have the ability to perform next day service for smaller issues while longer term projects must be scheduled in advance.

    Are you willing to work on-site?

    Our service is primarily provided on-site at businesses and homes for clients. So yes, we work on-site. Moreover, we're willing to work on-site daily for certain projects like installing networking equipment, smart home/business automation, or devops/IT outsourcing.
